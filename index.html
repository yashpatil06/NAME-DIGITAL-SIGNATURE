How It Works

🎯 What is a Keyboard Signature?

Your signature is created by connecting the letters of your name based on their positions on a QWERTY keyboard layout. Each letter maps to a specific key position, creating a unique path that represents your name visually.

🎨 Customization Options

  • Color: Choose any color for your signature line
  • Thickness: Adjust the line width from 1-20px
  • Background: Transparent, black, or white background
  • Points: Show dots at each letter position
  • Labels: Display the letters above their positions

💡 Tips

  • Longer names create more complex signatures
  • Try different letter combinations for unique patterns
  • Export as PNG for high-quality images
  • Transparent backgrounds work great on any surface
